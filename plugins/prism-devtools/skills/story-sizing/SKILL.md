Manage story sizing using PROBE (PROxy-Based Estimation) across three modes.

## Steps

1. Identify the sizing mode: **estimate** (new story), **calibrate** (10+ completed stories), or **resize** (story too large/small)
2. Follow the relevant mode in [instructions.md](./reference/instructions.md)
3. Update story file(s) with new estimates and tracking fields

See [instructions.md](./reference/instructions.md) for detailed steps and examples.
